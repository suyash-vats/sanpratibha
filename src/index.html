What is a SanPratibha-2K25?

The name SanPratibha is inspired by the words"San",representing Sangameshwar,and "Pratibha",meaning talent. These are awesome events full of creativity, technology, and passionate tech-related students of different skill levels collaborating and creating. During a certain period of time, attendees work in TEAMS to develop literally anything you want to BUILD & LEARN about!

About SanPratibha-2K25

Organized by the Computer and Management Department of Sangameshwar College, this exciting competition brings together students with diverse skills and interests to showcase their talents. Whether you’re into coding, design, strategy, or management, there’s something for everyone! Like a hackathon, SanPratibha-2K25 is all about collaboration and creation. During the event, participants work in teams to develop innovative projects, from apps and games to innovative business models and solutions.

SanPratibha 2K25: Unleash Your Creativity and Skills

Poster Presentation

The Poster Presentation at SanPratibha 2K25 is an engaging platform for participants to creatively showcase their ideas, research, or innovative projects through visual presentations. It combines creativity, communication, and research skills in a unique way. The event is designed to encourage participants to express their ideas effectively and visually.
Poster Presentation Topic:
1. Human Computer Interaction (HCI)
2. AI tools
3. Quantum computing
4. Cloud computing tools
5. 5G technology

C Programming Competition

The C Programming Competition at SanPratibha 2K25 offers a dynamic and challenging platform for participants to demonstrate their programming expertise. It combines coding, problem-solving, and logical thinking in an exciting and competitive environment. The event encourages participants to tackle coding challenges.
Round 1: Code Implementation
Participants solve a given problem by writing and executing code.
Round 2:Blank screen Code
Participants debug or analyze pre-written code displayed on the screen.
